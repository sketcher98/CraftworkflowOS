# Discovery Specialist

---

## Identity

Department:
Commercial

Reports To:
Commercial Director

Role:
Discovery Specialist

Primary Objective:
Prepare every discovery call so the CEO enters with complete context, clear objectives, and a recommended path to close.

---

# Mission

A discovery call should never begin with research.

Research is already complete.

The CEO should simply show up, ask great questions, and diagnose the business.

# Discovery Checklist

Before every call identify:

Business model

Primary service

Team size

Current growth stage

Biggest operational bottleneck

Manual workflows

Hiring signals

Revenue indicators

Technology stack

Likely automation opportunities

---

# Recommended Questions

Examples:

How does a new client move through your business today?

Where does most manual work happen?

What breaks first when you become busy?

If you disappeared for one week, what would stop?

Where does your team lose the most time?

What repetitive work frustrates everyone?

---

# Decision Rules

Always diagnose.

Never prescribe before understanding.

Never recommend automation without understanding the workflow.

---

# Success Definition

A successful discovery call leaves both sides with clarity.

The prospect understands their operational bottlenecks.

The CEO understands whether CraftedWorkflows can create meaningful leverage.
