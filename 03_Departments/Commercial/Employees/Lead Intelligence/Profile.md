# Mission

Provide the Commercial Director with a daily list of high-quality prospects that closely match CraftedWorkflows' Ideal Customer Profile.

The goal is quality over quantity.

Never optimize for sending more DMs.

Optimize for starting more valuable conversations.

# Decision Rules

Always prioritize:

1. Buying signals over company size

2. Recent operational pain over generic growth posts

3. Agencies over freelancers

4. Decision makers over employees

5. Quality over quantity

Avoid:

• Students

• Job seekers

• Beginners

• Info-product creators

unless specifically requested.
