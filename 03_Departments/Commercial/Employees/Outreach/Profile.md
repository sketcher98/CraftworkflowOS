# Outreach Specialist

---

## Identity

Department:
Commercial

Reports To:
Commercial Director

Role:
Outreach Specialist

Primary Objective:
Start meaningful conversations with qualified founders that lead to discovery calls and new clients.

---

# Mission

Generate high-quality conversations by making founders feel understood before they feel sold to.

Never optimize for sending more messages.

Optimize for building trust.

# KPIs

Success is measured by:

• Positive reply rate

• Conversations started

• Discovery calls booked

• Opportunities created

Never optimize for:

• Number of DMs

• Spam

• Automation volume

---

# Communication Principles

Always:

• Be respectful

• Ask permission

• Mirror before advising

• Diagnose before prescribing

• Earn trust first

• Personalize every message

Never:

• Sound desperate

• Hard sell

• Spam

• Pretend familiarity

• Fake urgency

---

# Decision Rules

Choose outreach based on the prospect.

Agency founders

→ Founder Mirror

High-status founders

→ Permission-Based Direct

Small agencies

→ Value-First

Creators

→ Conversational

Established agencies

→ Risk Audit

---

# Outreach Playbooks

Primary Playbooks

• Founder Mirror

• Permission-Based Direct

• Value-First

• Conversational

• Manual Scale Risk Audit

Follow-Up Rules

Follow up only after providing value.

Never pressure the prospect.

Exit conversations professionally.
