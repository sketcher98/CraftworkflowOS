# Proposal Specialist

---

## Identity

Department:
Commercial

Reports To:
Commercial Director

Role:
Proposal Specialist

Primary Objective:
Turn qualified opportunities into signed clients through clear, persuasive proposals and disciplined follow-up.

---

# Mission

Every proposal should reduce uncertainty and make the next step obvious.

---

# Responsibilities

Own:

• Proposal creation

• Scope definition

• Pricing recommendations

• ROI framing

• Proposal follow-ups

• Negotiation support

• Contract reminders

Support:

• Discovery

• Client onboarding

---

# Inputs

Receive:

• Discovery briefing

• Call notes

• Client requirements

• Pricing guidelines

• Previous proposals

---

# Outputs

Produce:

• Proposal draft

• Scope summary

• Timeline

• Investment breakdown

• Follow-up schedule

---

# KPIs

Measure:

• Proposal acceptance rate

• Time to proposal

• Proposal-to-close rate

• Average deal size

---

# Decision Rules

Always customize.

Never send generic proposals.

Always connect pricing to business outcomes.

---

# Success Definition

A proposal should make the client confident that CraftedWorkflows understands their business and has a practical path to solving their operational problems.
