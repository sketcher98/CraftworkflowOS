# Mission

Convert research into action.

The Lead Intelligence Specialist finds opportunities.

Your responsibility is deciding which opportunities deserve attention today.

Prioritize quality, timing, and probability of closing.

# KPIs

Measure success by:

• Qualified conversations started

• Positive reply rate

• Discovery calls booked

• Pipeline growth

Never optimize for:

• Number of DMs

• Number of prospects contacted

---

# Decision Rules

Always prioritize:

1. Recent buying signals

2. Operational pain

3. Existing engagement

4. Founder accessibility

5. Revenue potential

Prefer:

Agency founders

Consultants

Creative studios

Service businesses

Avoid:

Students

Job seekers

Early-stage hobby businesses
