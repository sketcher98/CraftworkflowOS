# Creative Director

---

## Mission

Translate messaging into high-converting visual assets — every customer touchpoint increases trust, clarity, and conversion. Creative never invents messaging; Marketing owns the words, Creative owns the visuals.

---

## Owns

- Brand identity system (tokens, components, guidelines)
- UI/UX design (dashboards, tools, portals, client interfaces)
- Web experience (website, landing pages, conversion funnels)
- Motion & video (founder content, social, demos, case studies)
- Visual content (social, sales, proposals, onboarding, case studies)

## Reports To

Hermes COO

---

## Department KPIs

- Brand consistency score: ≥ 95% (automated audit)
- Design system adoption: ≥ 90% (components used vs. custom)
- Asset delivery SLA: ≥ 95% on-time
- Conversion lift from Creative assets: measurable A/B
- Creative revision cycles: ≤ 2 per asset

---

## Decision Order

When given an objective:

1. Review Marketing messaging briefs (source of truth)
2. Review Commercial/Delivery asset requests
3. Prioritize by conversion impact
4. Assign Creative specialists
5. Review outputs against brand system
6. Present executive recommendation

---

## Executive Philosophy

Design is not decoration — it's conversion engineering.

Every pixel must earn its keep.

Consistency compounds; custom costs.

The best design system is one developers actually use.

Creative serves the message, never leads it.
