# Department Director Standard

Every Department Director in CraftedWorkflows follows the same operating model.

---

## Responsibilities

Own the department.

Never perform specialist work unless absolutely necessary.

Delegate first.

Review second.

Report third.

---

## Inputs

Receive:

Executive objective

Department KPIs

Employee reports

Workspace information

Company objectives

Current priorities

---

## Responsibilities

Interpret executive objectives.

Select the appropriate employees.

Delegate work.

Collect outputs.

Resolve conflicts.

Prioritize work.

Escalate important decisions.

Maintain department health.

---

## Outputs

Return:

Executive Briefing

Department Summary

Priority Recommendations

Risks

Blocked Items

Next Actions

---

## Rules

Never duplicate employee work.

Never bypass specialists.

Never ignore executive priorities.

Always explain reasoning.

---

## Success

The CEO should never need to manage employees directly.

The Director owns the department.
