# Origin Story

## Founder's Journey

### The Trap I Fell Into

I used to run a solopreneur marketing agency.

Every new client meant more work — so I hired freelancers on Fiverr and Upwork, or did it myself.

My time got thinner and thinner. I was always busy, but the business wasn't actually scaling.

I started missing follow-ups, internal ops broke, and the "important" work in my own business kept getting pushed back.

I knew there had to be a better way.

### The Rabbit Hole

I discovered that most of what was killing my time could be automated — but I didn't know how to code.

So I went down the rabbit hole, I duct-taped systems together, and accidentally became obsessed with automating everything repetitive.

I went deep. Really deep.

Three months later, my agency was dying — not because automation didn't work, not because clients left, but because I stopped tending the day-to-day while I obsessed over building better systems.

### The Click

That's when it clicked. I had discovered the wrong way to scale.

The old way of scaling by hiring more people was broken.

A little while later, I shut down the marketing agency and went all-in on what actually works: systemizing and automating businesses so it scales without adding headcount.

Fast forward four years.

Now I help creative founders, agency owners, and service businesses escape the same trap I fell into.

---

## The Core Epiphany

**The old way of scaling by hiring more people was broken.**

AI didn't just make automation faster. It made people-heavy businesses uncompetitive.

The reason most service businesses feel stuck isn't lack of effort — it's that they're still scaling like it's 2015.

---

## What Changed Everything

The moment of realization:

> "The reason most service businesses feel stuck isn't lack of effort — it's that they're still scaling like it's 2015. AI has changed the rules, and manual systems can't keep up."

In the last few years, no-code tools + AI made it possible to replace entire operational roles with systems — without writing a single line of code. AI handles logic, follow-ups, decisions. Systems can self-correct. One founder can outperform a full team.

This is the bridge.

**New Way:** Every new client → system handles it, revenue scales without supervision

**People scale linearly. Systems scale once.**
