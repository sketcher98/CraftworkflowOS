# Target Market

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Primary Segments

| Segment | Revenue Range | Team Size | Pain Level | Buying Power |
|---------|---------------|-----------|------------|--------------|
| **Agency Owners** | $5k–$100k/mo | 2–15 people | High | High |
| **Consultants** | $10k–$50k/mo | Solo–5 people | Medium-High | High |
| **Creative Founders** | $10k–$30k/mo | Solo–5 people | Medium | Medium |
| **Service Businesses** | $10k–$100k/mo | 2–10 people | High | High |

### Sweet Spot

- **Revenue**: $10k–$50k/month
- **Team**: 2–10 people
- **Stage**: Growing but operationally strained
- **Mindset**: Ready for AI, overwhelmed by manual work

---

## Detailed Personas

### 1. The Overwhelmed Agency Owner
- **Revenue**: $15k–$40k/mo
- **Team**: 3–8 people (mix of employees + freelancers)
- **Pain**: Every new client adds chaos; delivery breaks; follow-ups slip
- **Trigger**: "Booked out but overwhelmed", "Hiring to keep up"
- **Wants**: Predictable delivery, fewer fires, time to sell

### 2. The Scaling Consultant
- **Revenue**: $20k–$50k/mo
- **Team**: Solo or 1–2 contractors
- **Pain**: Everything routes through them; can't step away
- **Trigger**: "Can't step away", "Follow-ups slipping"
- **Wants**: Self-running pipeline, freedom to think

### 3. The Creative Studio Founder
- **Revenue**: $10k–$30k/mo
- **Team**: 2–5 creatives + PM
- **Pain**: Creative work gets squeezed by ops/admin
- **Trigger**: "Onboarding is a mess", "Handoffs break"
- **Wants**: Creative freedom, predictable ops

---

## Qualification Criteria

### Must Have (All Required)
- [ ] Service business (agency, consulting, creative, coaching)
- [ ] $5k+ monthly recurring revenue
- [ ] Actively feeling operational pain
- [ ] Open to AI/automation
- [ ] Decision-maker (founder/owner)

### Strong Signals (At Least 2)
- [ ] "Booked out" or "fully booked" posts
- [ ] Hiring announcements
- [ ] "Can't step away" language
- [ ] "Hiring to keep up" posts
- [ ] Delivery bottleneck complaints
- [ ] Manual follow-up complaints

### Disqualifiers
- Pre-revenue / pre-launch
- Info-product creators only
- "DIY automation" seekers
- Not the decision maker
- No budget / no urgency

| **Booking out / full pipeline** | Medium | 30–90 days |
| **Burnout signals** | High | Immediate |
| **New client announcement** | Medium | 30 days |

## Objection Patterns by Segment

| Segment | Top Objection | Response Strategy |
|---------|---------------|-------------------|
| Agency ($20k+) | "We have people for this" | Show cost of supervision vs. systems |
| Agency ($5k–$15k) | "Can't afford it" | Cost of inaction > cost of systems |
| Consultant | "I can build this myself" | Architecture vs. execution |
| Creative | "I'm not technical" | No-code / we build it |
