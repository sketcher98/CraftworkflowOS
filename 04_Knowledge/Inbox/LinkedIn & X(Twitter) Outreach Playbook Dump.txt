💬 STEP 2 — PUBLIC REPLIES (15 MIN)

Reply to 5–10 posts max.

Use:

1 sentence mirror

OR

1 diagnostic question

Nothing else.

Examples (rotate only these):

“What part of this feels most risky right now?”

“This reads like something that works… until it doesn’t.”

“The hard part is this becomes permanent if nothing changes.”

Rules:

No links

No “I help”

No advice stacks

Stop after 1 reply

👉 Your reply should make other people nod, not impress.

⏳ STEP 3 — WAIT (IMPORTANT)

Do nothing for 1–3 hours.

Why:

Let them respond publicly

Let others pile on

Let the emotion mature

This increases DM acceptance later.

📥 STEP 4 — DM ENTRY (15 MIN)

Now DM only people who meet ONE of these:

Replied to you

Liked your reply

Clearly emotionally exposed in the post

For Anger

That’s it.
