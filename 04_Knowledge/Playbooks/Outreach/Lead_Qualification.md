# Lead Qualification

How to qualify leads before investing time in calls.

---

## Buying Signals (Strong → Buy Now)

| Signal | Urgency | Action |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **Hiring** | Critical | DM within 24h (Flow 2/5) |
| **Growing team** | High | DM within 48h (Flow 2) |
| **Booked out** | High | DM within 48h (Flow 1/5) |
| **New clients** | Medium | DM within 72h (Flow 1) |
| **Expanding** | Medium | DM within 72h (Flow 1) |
| **Operations breaking** | Critical | DM immediately (Flow 5) |

---

## Weak Signals (Nurture Only)

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Motivational content | Nurture / ignore |
| Lifestyle content | Nurture / ignore |
| Personal branding | Nurture / ignore |
| Generic business advice | Nurture / ignore |

---

## Pain Signals (What to Listen For)

### Strong Pain (Ready to Buy)
- "Everything runs through me"
- "Can't step away"
- "Hiring to keep up"
- "Follow-ups falling through"
- "Delivery breaking"
- "Onboarding is a mess"
- "Things are breaking"
- "Manual work everywhere"

### Medium Pain (Warm)
- "Busy but overwhelmed"
- "Growing pains"
- "Process problems"
- "Burnout"
- "Scaling challenges"

### Weak Pain (Nurture)
- Educational posts
- General founder content
- "Building in public"
- Motivational content

---

## Buying Signals in Conversation

### Green Lights (Move to Call)
- "Everything runs through me"
- "Manual work everywhere"
- "Can't step away"
- "Afraid to add more clients"
- "Follow-ups falling through"
- "Things falling through cracks"
- "Hard to delegate"

### Yellow Lights (Qualify More)
- "We use tools but..."
- "Tried automation before"
- "Team handles it mostly"
- "Not sure what to automate"

### Red Lights (Disqualify)
- "Things are fine"
- "We use tools, things are fine"
- "Just looking"
- "DIY mindset"
- "Info product sellers"

## Lead Scoring Matrix

| Criteria | Points |
|----------|--------|
| **Revenue $10k–$100k/mo** | +30 |
| **Team 2-15 people** | +25 |
| **Strong pain signals (3+)** | +20 |
| **Recent hiring/growth** | +15 |
| **Engaged with content** | +10 |
| **Replied to DM** | +10 |
| **Asked for call** | +20 |

**Score ≥ 70** → Book call immediately
**Score 50-69** → Nurture, follow up
**Score < 50** → Low priority, nurture only

## Disqualification Criteria

| Criteria | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Pre-revenue / pre-launch | Disqualify |
| Info-product only | Disqualify |
| Not decision maker | Ask for intro to founder |
| No budget / no urgency | Nurture only |
| "DIY automation" mindset | Send resources, don't call |
| "We'll do it in-house" | Ask: remove self or reduce pain? |

## Qualification Questions (DM / Call)

### Must Ask
1. "Walk me through what happens from new lead → client delivery."
2. "What are you still personally involved in that shouldn't require you?"
3. "How many hours a week does this pull you back in?"
4. "What breaks when you're busy or unavailable?"
4. "What still needs manual follow-up?"
5. "What breaks when you're busy or unavailable?"

### Probe Deep
- "What are you still personally involved in that shouldn't require you?"
- "How many hours are you personally involved then?"
- "Where do things slow down?"
- "What still needs manual follow-up?"
- "What breaks when you're busy or unavailable?"

### The Test Point
> "So what I'm hearing is — the business doesn't really break because of volume… it breaks because everything still routes through you. Sound accurate?"

If yes → green light. Keep control.
If no → disqualify or nurture.

## Lead Status Pipeline

| Stage | Criteria | Next Action |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| **New** | DM sent, no reply | Follow up Day 2, 5, 10 |
| **Engaged** | Replied, conversation started | Qualify with questions |
| **Qualified** | Pain confirmed, budget/timing | Book audit call |
| **Call Booked** | Calendar confirmed | Send pre-call sequence |
| **Call Complete** | Call done, decision pending | Send recap email |
| **Proposal Sent** | Proposal sent | Follow up 24h, 48h |
| **Closed Won** | Signed/paid | Onboard |
| **Closed Lost** | Said no | Nurture quarterly |
| **Nurture** | Not ready | Quarterly check-in |

## Daily Qualification Routine

**Morning (5 min):**
- Review new replies
- Score new leads
- Move qualified to "Book Call"

**During DMs:**
- Ask qualification questions naturally
- Listen for green/yellow/red signals
- Score in real-time

**Evening (5 min):**
- Update pipeline
- Schedule calls for qualified
- Archive unqualified
