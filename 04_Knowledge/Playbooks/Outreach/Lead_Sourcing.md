# Lead Sourcing

Where to find leads and how to source them systematically.

---

## Primary Sources (Priority Order)

### 1. LinkedIn Comment Mining (Highest ROI)

**Source profiles:**
- Agency founders who post opinions
- Agency advisors/operators (Karl Sakas, Jason Swenk, etc.)
- Mid-size agency CEOs (10-100 people)
- Agency brand pages (Accenture Song, VaynerMedia, etc.)

**Method:**
1. Pick 3-5 source profiles daily
2. Open last 2-3 posts
3. Scan comments for: founders, agency owners, ops pain language
3. DM commenters using Flow 1 (Founder Mirror) or Flow 5 (Risk Audit)

**Profiles to start with:**
- Chris Walker (Refine Labs)
- Jason Swenk (Agency Coach)
- Karl Sakas (Agency advisor)
- Peep Laja (CXL / Wynter)
- Ross Simmonds (Foundation Marketing)
- Dave Gerhardt (Exit Five)
- John Doherty (Credo)
- Tom Hunt (Fame)
- Ben Aston (Black & White Zebra)
- Ryan Stewart (Webris)

---

### 2. LinkedIn Search (Direct)

**Search combos:**
- Title: "Founder" OR "CEO" + Industry: "Marketing" + Keywords: "agency"
- Company size: 11-50 or 51-200
- Industry: "Marketing & Advertising"

**Profile patterns to look for:**
- "Founder @ [Agency Name]"
- "CEO — Digital Marketing Agency"
- "Managing Director, Creative Agency"
- Posts with actual comments (not just likes)

**Build list of 30-50, rotate daily.**

---

### 3. Twitter/X Search

**Bio keywords:**
- "Agency owner", "Founder @", "CEO @", "Studio", "Studio founder", "Agency Founder", "Founder @ agency", "CEO @ agency", "co-founder @ agency", "Agency CRO"

**Search queries:**
- "hiring again", "need to hire", "looking for a VA", "hiring a PM", "growing the team", "expanding the team"
- "booked out", "fully booked", "pipeline is full", "more clients than"
- "client delivery is", "onboarding is", "handoffs are", "delivery bottleneck", "ops are breaking", "internal ops"
- "follow ups are", "missed follow ups", "manual follow ups", "CRM is a mess"
- "working weekends", "can't step away", "business runs me"

**Engagement strategy:**
- DM within 24-48 hours of signal
- Use Flow 2 (Permission) or Flow 5 (Risk Audit)

---

### 4. Comment Mining (Pain Posts)

**Search for posts mentioning pain, then mine replies:**
- "Same here", "Me too", "We're dealing with this too", "This is us"
- DM those commenters (Flow 1 or 3)

---

### 5. LinkedIn Brand Pages (Sleeper)

**Search pages:** Accenture Song, VaynerMedia, Wieden+Kennedy, Ogilvy, AKQA, R/GA, Huge, MediaMonks, DEPT®, BBDO

**You don't DM the brand. You DM the commenters.**

Commenters = smaller agency owners, freelancers turning agency, ops managers.

---

## Who to Avoid (Hard No's)

- "LinkedIn Growth Gurus"
- Personal branding coaches
- Solo consultants selling info
- People posting only motivational carousels
- Anyone saying "DM me if you want to scale"

They attract the wrong commenters.

| Activity | Target |
|----------|--------|
| Source profiles | 3-5 |
| Posts scanned | 10-15 |
| Comments mined | 5-10 |
| DMs sent | 5-10 |
| Conversations started | 3-7 |

---

## Lead Qualification Checklist

Before DMing, verify:
- [ ] Founder/owner (not employee)
- [ ] Agency/consulting/service business
- [ ] 2-15 people (or solo scaling)
- [ ] Active in last 30 days
- [ ] Pain signals present
- [ ] Not info-product seller
- [ ] Not "growth guru"

## Lead Enrichment (Before Call)

Before any call, research:
- Company website (services, team page)
- LinkedIn (team size, recent posts)
- Tools they use (check website footer, job posts)
- Recent news/funding
- Competitors

Add to CRM/notes before call.
