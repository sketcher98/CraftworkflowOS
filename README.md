# CraftworkflowOS

> The internal operating system of CraftedWorkflows.

CraftworkflowOS is the centralized operating system used to run CraftedWorkflows.

It combines company documentation, operational knowledge, decision frameworks, current business state, projects, clients, and AI workflows into one organized workspace.

Unlike traditional documentation, CraftworkflowOS is designed to be used by both humans and AI agents working together.

---

## Purpose

The goal of CraftworkflowOS is to make the business easier to operate, easier to improve, and less dependent on any single person.

Everything inside this repository exists to help answer one question:

> "How should CraftedWorkflows operate today?"

---

## Core Principles

* The company is documented before it is automated.
* Systems are preferred over manual repetition.
* Decisions should follow documented principles.
* AI assists people—it does not replace accountability.
* Documentation is the source of truth.

---

## Folder Structure

00_System — System configuration and startup files.

01_CEO — Company mission, strategy, decision principles, and executive documents.

02_COO — Hermes identity and current company state.

03_Departments — Department-specific knowledge and future AI employees.

04_Knowledge — SOPs, documentation, skills, and company knowledge.

05_Operations — Operational processes, logs, workflows, and automations.

06_Clients — Client information and delivery records.

07_Projects — Active and completed internal projects.

08_Command_Center — Inbox, daily briefings, alerts, reviews, and executive decision support.

09_Archive — Historical records.

---

## Source of Truth

CraftworkflowOS is the authoritative source for company knowledge and current operational state.

Long-term memory systems (Graph Memory, Episodic Memory, Semantic Memory, etc.) provide context but never override documented company knowledge.

---

## AI Compatibility

CraftworkflowOS is designed to work with multiple AI systems.

Current executive:

* Hermes (Chief Operating Officer)

Future AI employees may include engineering, design, marketing, research, and operations specialists while sharing the same operating system.
